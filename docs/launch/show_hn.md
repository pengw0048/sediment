# Show HN: PKE - Close the learning loop on what AI did for you

I wrote this because I noticed myself outsourcing more and more thinking to AI
tools, and later realizing I could not reproduce things I had "done" with AI
help. PKE is a local-first knowledge engine that observes your own AI
interactions, extracts the concepts and skills involved, tracks what you can do
with AI versus unaided, and brings you back later through short review sessions
or optional Socratic nudges.

Everything runs on your machine. SQLite plus a local graph view and local
embeddings. No telemetry, no SaaS, no payment processing. AGPL.
